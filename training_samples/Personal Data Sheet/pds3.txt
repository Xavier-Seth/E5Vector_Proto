CS Form No. 212 (Revised 2017)
PERSONAL DATA SHEET (PDS)

I. PERSONAL INFORMATION
- Surname, First Name, Middle Name
- Date of Birth (mm/dd/yyyy), Place of Birth
- Citizenship, Sex, Civil Status
- Residential Address, Permanent Address
- IDs: GSIS, PAG-IBIG, PhilHealth, SSS, TIN, Agency Employee No.
- Contact: Telephone, Mobile, Email

II. FAMILY BACKGROUND
- Spouse: Name, Occupation, Employer, Business Address
- Children: Full Names, Birthdates
- Father’s Name
- Mother’s Maiden Name

III. EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND
- Elementary, Secondary, College, Graduate Studies
- School Name, Degree/Course, Period of Attendance, Year Graduated
- Units Earned, Scholarships/Honors

IV. CIVIL SERVICE ELIGIBILITY
- Career Service / Board / Bar / Special Eligibility
- Rating, Date of Exam, Place, License Number

V. WORK EXPERIENCE
- Inclusive Dates
- Position Title, Department/Agency/Office/Company
- Monthly Salary, Salary/Job/Pay Grade
- Status of Appointment
- Government Service (Yes/No)

VI. VOLUNTARY WORK
- Organization Name & Address
- Inclusive Dates, Number of Hours
- Position / Nature of Work

VII. LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT
- Title of Program
- Inclusive Dates, Hours, Type
- Conducted/Sponsored by

VIII. OTHER INFORMATION
- Special Skills & Hobbies
- Non-Academic Distinctions
- Membership in Associations

REFERENCES
- Name, Address, Telephone No.

Government Issued ID
- ID/License/Passport No.
- Date/Place of Issuance
- Signature, Date, Right Thumbmark, Person Administering Oath
