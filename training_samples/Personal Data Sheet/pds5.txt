CS Form No. 212 (Revised 2017) – PERSONAL DATA SHEET

I. PERSONAL INFORMATION
- Surname, First Name, Middle Name
- Date of Birth, Place of Birth
- Citizenship, Sex, Civil Status
- Residential & Permanent Address
- IDs: GSIS, PAG-IBIG, PhilHealth, SSS, TIN
- Contact Details

II. FAMILY BACKGROUND
- Spouse (Name, Occupation, Employer)
- Children (Full Names, Birthdates)
- Father’s Name
- Mother’s Maiden Name

III. EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND
- Elementary, Secondary, College, Graduate Studies
- School Name, Degree/Course, Period of Attendance
- Year Graduated, Honors
